Base64 Converter

How to use

  1. Paste your text or Base64 string in the input area.
  2. Click "Encode to Base64" or "Decode from Base64".
  3. Result appears instantly below.

Examples

  • Input: hello → Output: aGVsbG8=
  • Input: aGVsbG8= → Output: hello

Common mistakes

Decoding invalid Base64 strings will throw an error. Ensure your string uses valid characters only.
